The video outlines three specific limitations of using ChatGPT directly for equity research that necessitate the development of a custom tool (1:19:47 - 1:20:42):

Inefficient workflow: The process of manually copying and
 pasting individual articles into a chat interface is
 tedious and time-consuming for professional equity research
  analysts (1:19:49 - 1:20:01).

Lack of aggregate knowledge: Analysts need to perform queries across
multiple documents simultaneously. ChatGPT does not provide an
integrated knowledge base that can search and synthesize information
 spread across various different articles (1:20:04 - 1:20:29).

Word and token constraints: ChatGPT has strict limits on the volume of text
it can process at one time. This makes it unsuitable for analyzing large
amounts of source documentation required for thorough research (1:20:33 - 1:20:42).
